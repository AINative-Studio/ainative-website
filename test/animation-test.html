Implementation Details

  • slide-in-right: Fades in from 20px right, 0.5s ease-out
  • slide-in-left: Fades in from 20px left, 0.5s ease-out
  • scale-in: Fades in with scale from 0.95, 0.3s ease-out
  • Accessibility: All animations respect prefers-reduced-motion

Usage in Next.js Components

<div className="animate-slide-in-right">
  Content slides in from the right
</div>

<div className="animate-slide-in-left">
  Content slides in from the left
</div>

<div className="animate-scale-in">
  Content scales up smoothly
</div>
